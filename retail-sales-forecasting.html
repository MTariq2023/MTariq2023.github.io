Retail Sales Forecasting & Inflation Trends

U.S. Retail & Inflation Analysis with Tableau

Project Overview

This project analyzes U.S. retail sales data alongside CPI inflation rates to produce both nominal and real (inflation-adjusted) sales trends. Using Tableau, the dashboard visualizes monthly retail patterns, highlighting long-term shifts in consumer spending.

Tech Stack: Python (Pandas), Tableau, CSV/XLSX datasets
